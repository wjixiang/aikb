## Individual burden of disease

The direct costs of the disease can potentially be estimated as pharmacological, non-pharmacological or surgery expenses; however, the impact of their investment on the individual as a result of reduced pain, improved functionality and HRQoL is challenging to quantify monetarily. The most disabling symptom a person with OA experiences is pain 235 . Pain, stiffness and limitation of joint movement reduce HRQoL.
